Behavioral Economics & Decision Engineering (BEDE)

FOR CFOs & Finance Teams, Company Executives and Startups & Small Businesses WHO need to improve budgeting accuracy, make more informed investment decisions, identify potential financial risks, and take proactive steps. THE BEDE (Behavioral Economics & Decision Engineering) is a financial analysis & forecasting software THAT allows you to analyze company finances to predict spending needed for profitability or stabilization. UNLIKE SAP Integrated Business Planning and Oracle Fusion Cloud Supply Chain Planning, the focus of BEDE is being user-friendly and serving everyone who wants to manage their business resources. BEDE achieves its goal by having an easy-to-understand and navigate interface.
